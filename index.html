ABOUT

We are a club that encourages and motivates students who are interested in Computer Science to connect with each other through community engagement and tech events.

What & Why?

We are a club focused on fostering tech-based community engagement and networking opportunities. We have many events and activities for you to learn, socialize, and enhance your coding skillset.

Who?

We are accepting students of any major, gender, background and experience. We are devoted to making ourselves an inclusive club for students of all backgrounds while fostering our tight-knit community.

When & Where?

CS Connect BU's first semester as an official Computer Science club in Fall Semester 2020. Due to Boston University's LfA program, we will be hosting all of our events and activities virtually.
